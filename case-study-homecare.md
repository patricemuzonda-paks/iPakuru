# 🏥 Operational Efficiency Transformation  
**Client:** Love in Action Home Care Services (USA)  
**Role:** Business Process Consultant  
**Duration:** Apr 2024 – Nov 2025  

---

## 🔍 Challenge
The client operated with fragmented processes, inconsistent documentation, and limited visibility into performance metrics. This created operational delays, compliance risks, and inefficiencies across financial and care‑delivery workflows.

---

## 🛠 Approach
- Conducted full process mapping and gap analysis across financial and operational functions.  
- Designed KPIs and cost‑benefit models to quantify performance.  
- Authored new financial and operational procedure manuals.  
- Led change‑management workshops to ensure adoption of new processes.  
- Implemented automated reporting and performance‑tracking dashboards.

---

## ⭐ Results
- **~80% increase in operational efficiency** through redesigned workflows.  
- **90% staff adoption** of new procedures and controls.  
- Established continuous monitoring framework for key indicators.  

---

## 🧩 Key Skills Used
Process mapping • KPI design • Cost‑benefit analysis • Change management • Governance strengthening
