# 🧮 SME Audit & Advisory  
**Client:** Twoj Start Up Foundation  
**Role:** Auditor / Business Analyst  
**Duration:** Jun 2021 – Apr 2022  

---

## 🔍 Challenge
SMEs required independent assessments of financial and operational processes, with clear, actionable recommendations to strengthen internal controls and improve performance.

---

## 🛠 Approach
- Conducted financial and operational audits tailored to SME risk profiles.  
- Produced audit reports translating technical findings into practical recommendations.  
- Monitored corrective actions and supported implementation.

---

## ⭐ Results
- **95% implementation** of corrective actions.  
- Strengthened internal controls and improved operational discipline.  

---

## 🧩 Key Skills Used
Audit reporting • Financial analysis • Operational review • Risk assessment
