# 🏦 Regulatory Audit Coordination  
**Client:** Standard Chartered Bank  
**Role:** External Audit Analyst  
**Duration:** Oct 2023 – Feb 2024  

---

## 🔍 Challenge
The bank needed to meet strict KNF and EU regulatory expectations while reducing audit‑cycle delays and repeat findings. Documentation quality and workflow efficiency required improvement.

---

## 🛠 Approach
- Coordinated statutory and regulatory audits across multiple business units.  
- Streamlined audit workflows and documentation processes.  
- Enhanced remediation tracking and reporting for governance committees.  
- Ensured alignment with KNF supervisory expectations.

---

## ⭐ Results
- **40% faster audit cycle** through workflow optimization.  
- **100% remediation** of audit findings.  
- **25% reduction** in repeat non‑conformities.  

---

## 🧩 Key Skills Used
Regulatory compliance • KNF/EU requirements • Audit coordination • Risk reporting
