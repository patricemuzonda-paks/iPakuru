# 🧾 SOX & Internal Controls Optimization  
**Client:** Global Financial Institutions (EY GDS)  
**Role:** Senior Business Consultant  
**Duration:** Jun 2022 – Sep 2023  

---

## 🔍 Challenge
Clients required stronger internal controls, improved SOX compliance, and more efficient audit processes across large, complex financial environments.

---

## 🛠 Approach
- Led risk‑based audits across EMEIA for global banking clients.  
- Applied data analytics to test large transaction populations.  
- Identified control gaps and process inefficiencies.  
- Recommended governance enhancements aligned with international standards.  
- Mentored junior auditors and supported client proposals.

---

## ⭐ Results
- **~40% YoY reduction** in risk and cost for key clients.  
- **25% increase** in team productivity.  
- Strengthened governance and internal‑control maturity.  

---

## 🧩 Key Skills Used
SOX • ICFR • Data analytics • Risk assessment • Governance frameworks
