# 🏛 Bank-Wide Risk & Audit Leadership  
**Client:** Infrastructure Development Bank  
**Role:** Internal Audit Manager  
**Duration:** Apr 2014 – May 2021  

---

## 🔍 Challenge
The bank required a stronger risk‑based audit function to address credit, operational, and compliance risks, while improving governance and reducing fraud.

---

## 🛠 Approach
- Directed the internal audit function and designed risk‑based audit plans.  
- Evaluated credit, operational, and compliance risks across lending and treasury.  
- Reported audit results to senior management, audit committees, and regulators.  
- Coordinated with external auditors and supervisory bodies.

---

## ⭐ Results
- **60% reduction in fraud** across key processes.  
- **50% fewer compliance breaches**.  
- **100% adherence** to audit timelines and regulatory requirements.  

---

## 🧩 Key Skills Used
Risk‑based audit planning • Credit/operational risk • Governance • Regulatory reporting
